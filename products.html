Smart City / Transport

Modern cities are competing on how to most effectively use data to improve living standards and infrastructure, and potentially also generate revenue. Using Aircloak’s technology, they can:

  • Combine previously locked data sets on user level, like transportation use, carsharing, socio-demographic data, indoor movement analytics and create added intelligence
  • Absolutely protect citizens’ privacy. Build trust through the extreme level of oversight that Aircloak allows through patentpending technology.
  • Open up data for monetization or OpenData: use Aircloak’s analytics interfaces that ensure malicious queries are next to impossible.

Medical

Medical data are increasingly recorded electronically; however currently, privacy laws lead to data silos in different institutions and make quantitative and real time use of the records prohibitively difficult.

  • Safely share medical data analytics between institutions. Private / sensitive data legally never leave single hospitals, but data can be combined in a central location and safely analyzed
  • Compare patients’ records with similar records of all participating institutions, thereby unlocking new treatment possibilities
  • No new opt-in necessary; existing data sets can be used, if required.
